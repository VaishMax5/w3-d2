India Triumph's over Pakistan

India's 7-wicket victory over Pakistan in the ICC World Cup 2023, witnessed by over 1 lakh roaring fans at the Narendra Modi Stadium, marked the eighth consecutive triumph for India over their arch-rivals in World Cup history. This remarkable streak dates back to 1992, cementing India's dominance in these high-stakes clashes.

India's Asian Games Success

India won a record 107 medals at the 19th Asian Games in Hangzhou, China, with 28 gold, 38 silver, and 41 bronze medals. The Indian contingent's medal rush was led by track and field athletes and shooters, who won 29 and 22 medals respectively. Athletics saw a rich haul of six gold, 14 silver, and nine bronze medals.
